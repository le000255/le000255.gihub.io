About me

Hi! My name is Gia Thinh, and I invite you to call me Eddie. I am so excited to be a student of Interactive Media Design (IMD) program at Algonquin College. This is my 3rd year in Canada now. I find Ottawa a beautiful city; I am so in love with its peaceful atmosphere. The people here are awesome too!

Before residing in Canada, I used to be an English teacher; my students were kids from three to 12 years old. Oddly enough, something happened during my teaching journey which ignited an interest in web development and led me to IMD program as of today. You may be curious enough to ask: “How come?” If you do, let’s talk to find out.
