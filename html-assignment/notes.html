My Notes

My top five HTML tags

header tag:
used as a container of a page's title or a group of navigation links
h1 tag:
defines the title of the page
main tag:
used as a container of the page's primary content
article tag:
wraps around the whole body of reading texts (e.g., blog posts, news articles)
img tag:
used to display images in a document
